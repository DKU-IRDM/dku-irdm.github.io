Welcome to the IRDM Lab.

We are a research laboratory at Dankook University.

We study Information Retrieval (IR), Data Mining (DM), and a broad range of Machine Learning and Deep Learning topics, with a particular focus on Recommender Systems (RecSys) and Explainable AI (XAI).

---

Our research topics include, but are not limited to:

* Context-aware Sequential Recommendation
* Multi-modal Recommender Systems
* LLM-based Recommender Systems
* Text-to-SQL using LLMs
* Bias Detection and Correction in LLMs
* Interpreting Internal Model Behavior
* Model Lightweighting via Pruning and Distillation
